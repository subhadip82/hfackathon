Understand Your Health Policy

Everything you need to know before buying a health policy.

A health policy is an insurance plan that covers medical expenses such as hospitalization, surgery, and treatment due to illnesses or accidents.
Everyone, regardless of age or profession, should have a health policy. It ensures financial security during medical emergencies.
  • Cashless hospitalization
  • Tax benefits under Section 80D
  • Coverage for critical illnesses
  • Peace of mind for your family
ID proof, age proof, address proof, medical reports (if any), and passport-sized photos are usually needed.
  • Compare different insurers
  • Check hospital network coverage
  • Read policy terms carefully
  • Prefer lifelong renewability
It provides protection from sudden medical costs, ensures access to quality healthcare, and offers tax benefits.
The insured person or their family nominee can claim the benefits for eligible expenses as per the policy terms.
